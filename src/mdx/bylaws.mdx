## Article I: Name and Purpose

##### Section A - Name:

BTHS Repair the World

##### Section B - National Affiliation:

Repair the World, but the organization does not provide a set of rules and regulations to follow.

##### Section C - Purpose:

The purpose of this club shall be:

1. Bring people together for volunteer opportunities.
2. Educate people about an unjust society by working with those affected.
3. Helping everyone have equal opportunities, bit by bit.
4. Aiding those in need through service.

The purpose of this shall **NOT** be:

1. Encourage the Jewish religion or any religious alignment in any way; the organization says "Jews and their communities", which means everyone, as they personally declared in person.
2. Repairing the world is explicitly stated in Jewish religion, but it should really be a universal one. Do NOT use this to falsely claim we are a religiously aligned club.

##### Section D - Club Email:

This is the email that will be used to contact them. [bthsrepairtheworld@gmail.com](mailto:bthsrepairtheworld@gmail.com)

## Article II: Membership and Dues

##### Section A - Eligibility:

Membership shall be open to people who condemn bigotry, forms of supremacy and corrupt solutions to issues upon payment of the dues as outlined in Section B.
